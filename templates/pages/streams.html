About streams

The basics

A stream is basically a topic of conversation. You can create one through the mobile or desktop app, or via the website. You can make your stream private, so it’s accessible via its address, but not listed anywhere on the homepage, or added to our search index.

A stream can contain text (including links), photos from the web, your hard drive or your mobile, aswell as audio and video from the same places.

Join in the conversation

Other people can find your streams based on keywords or hashtags, and by location. They can also join your stream to report on the same story.
