About Networks

The basics

A Network is a collection of streams, bound together by a specific topic. Take for example a conference like Comic-Con or South by South West. They run over multiple days, and there’s always more than one thing to do at any time, whether it’s sitting in on a panel, enjoying a launch party or getting a book signed.

For events like these, it’s not practical to put everything into one stream, so you can use Networks to create streams that live in a specific address. This could be something.{{ request.network.parent.domain }}, or your own custom domain name.

Creating a Network

Networks will only be available for paid accounts, but while we’re getting those setup, if you’d like to create a Network for your next event, please email hello@meegloo.com and we can talk.
