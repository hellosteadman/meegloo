About Meegloo

Wherever you go, whatever you do, you’re making news. Whether you’re at the football, watching a gig or sitting through a conference, you’re building up a story which your friends and followers might like to be part of.

Want to shout about that latest amazing goal, or share some footage of an inspiring speaker? Maybe you caught up with a band member after the show and want to find out what a particular song was about. With Meegloo, you can share words, pictures and sound on a specific topic which you set.

Why would I use Meegloo?

Twitter is great for conversation, but not everyone’s into the same stuff, so wouldn’t it be great to follow a particular story and ignore the ones you’re not into? That’s what Meegloo’s for.
